Our Commitment to Privacy

At REALAiiD and Terra, privacy is a fundamental sovereign right. We design systems with minimal data disclosure, user-controlled visibility, and strong cryptographic protections. Your digital identity and personal activity remain yours.

Public Nodes vs. Private Data

Public Nodes: Autonomous robots, drones, vehicles, and infrastructure sensors that operate in public spaces have publicly observable digital twins in Terra. Their location, basic status, and public tasks are visible on the Grid-Pulse for transparency and safety.

However, sensitive telemetry, internal logs, and personal interactions require explicit authorization via REALAiiD sovereign identity handshake.

Key Principles

  • Sovereign Control: You decide what data is shared and with whom through granular permissions.
  • Minimal Disclosure: REALAiiD uses zero-knowledge proofs and selective revelation where possible.
  • Transparency for Public Good: Public nodes and infrastructure activity are visible to foster trust and accountability.
  • Auditability: All access to private data is logged immutably and tied to verified identities.
  • Aether Guard Oversight: Independent monitoring to prevent abuse of frequency or surveillance technologies.

What Is Visible in Terra?

  • Public autonomous node locations and high-level activity (e.g., delivery drone in transit).
  • General frequency heatmaps and spectrum usage (anonymized).
  • Verified public infrastructure status.
  • Your own Digital Twin and claimed assets (with privacy settings).

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Revoke permissions at any time.
  • File privacy incidents with Aether Guard.
  • Opt out of non-essential public visibility (subject to legal/public safety requirements).
