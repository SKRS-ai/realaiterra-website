What are Autonomous Zones & Ways (AVWs)?

AVWs are purpose-built physical and digital infrastructure corridors designed exclusively for autonomous robots, drones, and vehicles. They function as dedicated "robot highways" that operate separately from human traffic for maximum safety, efficiency, and performance.
